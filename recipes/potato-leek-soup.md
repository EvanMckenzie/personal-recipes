title: Potato Leek Soup
ingredients_key: potato-leek-soup
---

1. Cut leek in half length-wise.
2. Rinse leek to remove any silt between layers.
3. Cut each half of leek into crescent slices (ignore the dark green leafy part).
4. Peel potatoes and chop into rough 1 inch cubes.
5. In a large soup pot, melt 1 TBSP butter and add leek once foamy.
6. Cover pot and reduce heat to low. Let leek cook for 7 minutes.
7. After 7 minutes, add 4 CUPs of vegetable broth and cubed potatoes.
8. Cover pot and increase heat to medium-low. Stew for 25 minutes or until potatoes are soft and mashable.
9. Mash potatoes until the desired consistency. For a smoother consistency, you can also blend the soup.
10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
11. Garnish with parsely.
